Stellar Interactions with Protoplanetary Disks

My undergraduate senior thesis at Rice University revolved around determining if the asymmetries in protoplanetary disks we see in nature are caused by gravitational interactions with neighboring stars. The Gaia DR2, which was convieniently released after my graduation, is now being used to complete the project.
